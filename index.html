About

DJing has been in my blood since the late 80s/early 90s, and even though I don’t play live anymore, the passion for dance music has never gone away. This channel is all about the mixes I create from the music I love most — fresh tracks, record pool discoveries, festival-inspired energy, and sets I’d want to play loud myself.

I upload mixes across bass house, tech house, nu-disco, mainstage and hard house, shaped by a lifelong love of club culture and the EDM festival scene. Inspired by the atmosphere of events I still attend like Tomorrowland, AMF and Don’t Let Daddy Know, these mixes are made for anyone who loves big energy, strong flow and great dance music without the filler. I make them for me first — and share them for anyone else who wants to come along for the ride.

Latest Mix

If you love massive builds, euphoric melodies and a closing stretch that goes absolutely off, this one is made to hit loud. New Release

This mix is a straight-up club weapon — dirty basslines, rolling tech energy and big attitude from the first drop to the last. It kicks off with a slick rework of The Next Episode before diving headfirst into the electro punch of Discoteka and the euphoric lift of Greece 2000, setting the tone for a set that blends nostalgia, swagger and modern underground heat. From there, the pressure stays on with groove-heavy cuts like No Broke Boys, Addicted, RAW, Shut It Down and Keep It Burning, each one built to keep the floor moving. The middle section leans hard into chunky house rhythms and bold vocal hooks, with Turn The Volume Up, Fancy $hit, Bugatti and All Night Long bringing relentless late-night energy. By the time Mind Dimension, Out Of Space, Get Wild and Go Go Go land, the mix is in full stride — raw, punchy and made for peak-time club chaos. Fast, filthy and packed with groove, this is a no-nonsense set built to hit hard.
