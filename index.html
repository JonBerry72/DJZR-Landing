About DJZR

DJing has been in my blood since the late 80s and early 90s, and even though I don’t play live anymore, the passion for dance music has never gone away. This channel is all about the mixes I create from the music I love most — fresh tracks, record pool discoveries, festival-inspired energy, and sets I’d want to play loud myself.

I upload mixes across bass house, tech house, nu-disco, mainstage and hard house, shaped by a lifelong love of club culture and the EDM festival scene. Inspired by events I still attend like Tomorrowland, AMF and Don’t Let Daddy Know, these mixes are made for anyone who loves big energy, strong flow and great dance music without the filler. I make them for me first — and share them for anyone else who wants to come along for the ride.
